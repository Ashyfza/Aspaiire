This class is from the Islamic Education programme at the Institut Pendidikan Guru Kampus Keningau, and it is a cheerful and lively group of 26 classmates who share a strong sense of unity. We always support each other in both academic and personal matters, creating a positive and encouraging environment where no one feels left out. Beyond studying, we actively take part in many activities together, which strengthens our bond and makes our class feel more like a family than just a group of students.
